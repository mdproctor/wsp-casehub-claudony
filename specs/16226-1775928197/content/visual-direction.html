My recommendation: A — Deep Space Colony. The hex grid is the colony metaphor made visual — each cell is a session slot, lit nodes are active sessions. Electric cyan on void-black reads as premium and technical without being retro (C) or abstract (B). It gives the most flexibility for illustrations: you can draw actual colony organisms inside the hex structure, show a session map as a star chart, depict the controller Claude as the hub node. Direction B is beautiful but harder to make readable at small sizes. Direction C is distinctive but niche — the CRT aesthetic limits the audience.
